ABOUT US

J' Tuzba 29.11 Wedding Essentials is a business that offers gown rentals and provides wedding essentials. The business offers a variety of clothing used for special events such as weddings, birthdays, oath takings, etc. It also provide wedding or other event services and products such as photography and videography, catering, make -ups, flower arrangement, tokens and invitations.

The business is located at Stall 2081 2nd floor Maharlika Bldg, Baguio City. "J 29.11" means Jeremiah 29:11,a verse from the Bible. While Tuzba means health and beauty, . which serves as the name of the company, J'Tuzba 29.11. The business was recently established in February 2023. The initial motivation and reason for Starting the business are due to passion and skill of the owner. Also it is in demand especially after the restrictions caused by the pandemic, people wanted to have their dream event held without limitations.

The owners stated that the initial difficulties faced were insufficient funds, lack of supplies and equipment, and insufficient space especially for fitting room. The company overcame its initial difficulties through loans, and also general merchandise and dropping area as a side business. The company is able to generate income through this. With the use of the income and loans, the owners are able to expand their stall and little by little the owners are able to purchase supplies and equipment.
